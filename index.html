PLAN YOUR TRIP Travel Services 01 Trip Planning Custom itineraries built around how you actually want to travel. Flights, hotels, transfers, activities, handled from start to finish. 02 Immersive Itineraries These trips are built for travelers who want more than a highlights reel. Think small towns, slower days, and experiences shaped by local knowledge. 03 Local Experiences Real local recommendations, vetted connections, and experiences that don't show up on TripAdvisor. 04 Flights, Accommodation & Rental Cars Flight research, hotel sourcing, and booking handled for you. I match you with the right hotels in the right neighborhoods, often with complimentary perks through Fora and Virtuoso, including room upgrades, daily breakfast, and resort credits. I also have access to business-class flight and rental car rates unavailable to the public. 05 Price Monitoring I use a rate-tracking system that monitors refundable hotel bookings and alerts me when a price drops. When it does, I rebook at the lower rate. You pay less without having to think about it. 06 Groups, Weddings & Retreats Corporate retreats, weddings, multi-family vacations. I negotiate room blocks, review and manage contracts, source meeting and event space, and coordinate the countless logistical details.
